You can use the Land Access Request service(LARs) to view, Manage and request permission to access land from landowners, Managers and tenants around England.

You Can use the Service to:

  • Find out who oens and runs a piece of land
  • Create land access request letters and emails
  • View communications with landowners
  • Co-ordinate various survey visits across government bodies
  • Share information with other interested parties
  • Report or request landowner data
